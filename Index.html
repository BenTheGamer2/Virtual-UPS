About vUPS

vUPS is a virtual airline simulation built on real UPS Airlines data — real routes, real flight numbers, real block times and real aircraft. Every flight you dispatch shows up live for the whole community to see.

🗺
Real-Time World Map
Every active flight plotted live with great-circle arcs and animated plane positions. Click any marker for full flight details.
✈
Smart Route Generator
Pick your departure airport and aircraft — the system finds a realistic matching destination based on real block times and aircraft capability.
📋
SimBrief Integration
One click opens a full OFP — fuel planning, weather, routing, performance data — all pre-filled with your exact flight parameters.
🔄
Live Multi-User Sync
Dispatch a flight and every crew member worldwide sees it within seconds. Powered by Supabase real-time subscriptions.
🏦
Full VA Economics
Treasury, fleet purchases, hub operations, crew payroll and profit distribution. Run like a real airline boardroom.
📡
Scheduled Operations
AI pilots fly the real UPS published schedule 24/7 — real flight numbers, real departure times, real block times.
